Atmet is an AI automation platform that acts as a coworker — you describe what you want to automate, and Atmet's AI agent builds the workflow, connects your apps, and keeps it running around the clock. This page introduces how Atmet works, what makes it different from traditional automation tools, and where to go next.

## What Makes Atmet Different

Most automation tools ask you to drag and drop steps from a template gallery, map fields by hand, and maintain brittle logic as your apps change. Atmet takes a different approach: you describe the outcome you want in plain language, and the AI agent figures out the steps.

- **Capability-first, not template-driven.** Instead of browsing a library of pre-built templates, you tell Atmet what you need. The agent proposes a workflow plan, asks clarifying questions through smart input panels, and activates the automation — no visual wiring required.
- **Plain-language workflow creation.** Describe your workflow the way you'd explain it to a colleague: "When I receive an invoice email, save the attachment to Google Drive and notify me on Telegram." Atmet handles the rest.
- **The agent plans and executes.** Atmet's AI Core doesn't just generate a config file — it actively runs each step, monitors results, and surfaces human approval checkpoints when a sensitive action (like sending an email) is about to happen.

## Key Concepts

Before you dive in, here are the core building blocks you'll encounter across Atmet.

| Concept | What it is |
|---|---|
| **AI Core** | The conversational interface where you describe, iterate, and activate workflows |
| **Workflows** | Saved automation definitions that run 24/7 on triggers, without your presence |
| **Skills** | Reusable AI capability modules — system-built, workspace-shared, or personal |
| **Integrations** | The apps Atmet connects to (Gmail, Google Drive, Telegram, GitHub, and more) |
| **Workspace Memory** | Shared files, preferences, and context that persist across all agents in your workspace |

## Who Is Atmet For?

Atmet is built for anyone who spends time moving information between apps, following up on repetitive tasks, or coordinating work that could run itself.

- **Operations teams** who manage recurring processes — invoice handling, status updates, data routing — and want those processes to run without manual intervention.
- **Founders and small teams** who can't afford a dedicated automation engineer but need business-critical workflows to be reliable and observable.
- **Developers** who want to automate internal processes quickly, connect APIs without writing glue code, and give non-technical teammates a way to manage automations themselves.

If you find yourself doing the same multi-step task more than a few times a week, Atmet is designed for you.
